V-Surveillance: A Hybrid Deep Learning Framework for Real-Time Aerial Surveillance Using Drone Imagery

ESRGAN YOLO12M + SAHI Deep SORT UAV Edge Computing PyTorch

01 // Abstract

"Aerial surveillance using UAVs is important for safety and emergency response, as well as to monitor and regulate traffic. This research introduces V-Surveillance, an integrated approach to deep learning (Super Resolution, Object Detection & Multi-Object Tracking) for achieving situational awareness. V-Surveillance utilizes a three-stage pipeline that includes Super Resolution (ESRGAN), Fine Grained Object Detection (YOLO12M w/ SAHI), and Multi-Object Tracking (Deep SORT)."

"V-Surveillance’s performance was evaluated through a variety of aerial data sets that tested its ability to achieve accurate detection under varying lighting, altitude, and density conditions. The results showed that the framework achieved very high precision, recall, and sensitivity to small objects. V-Surveillance was designed for real time deployment to address the challenges of motion blur, camera instability and perspective variations, and has potential applications for urban monitoring, law enforcement, border security, traffic management and disaster response."

"V-Surveillance outperformed other frameworks on all four test datasets, with MAP@50 values of 0.967 on UAVDT, 0.966 on Spanish Roundabouts, 0.977 on Traffic Aerial Images, and 0.966 on Top View, thereby proving that the integration of multiple models enhances object detection capability in multiple conditions. These contributions have made V-Surveillance both capable of running in real time on standard UAV-class hardware, while also providing increased robustness in the challenging aerial environment."

02 // Three-Stage Deep Learning Pipeline

V-Surveillance uses three state-of-the-art models operating in sequence to resolve issues related to altitude shifts, camera blur, and perspective changes:

Stage 01

ESRGAN (Super Resolution)

Enhances details and textures in video frames captured from high altitudes, reconstructing essential features before detection.

Stage 02

YOLO12M + SAHI (Detection)

Applies Slicing Aided Hyper Inference (SAHI) alongside YOLO12M, optimizing spatial attention maps to detect extremely small structures.

Stage 03

Deep SORT (Tracking)

Associates objects across successive frames using motion-based Kalman filtering and descriptor distance matching to handle blur.
